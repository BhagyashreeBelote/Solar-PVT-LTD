Eco-Friendly Solar Panel

The solar panel is a collection of solar cells (or photovoltaic), which can be used
to generate electricity with the effect of photovoltaic. These cells are arranged in a grid-like
pattern on the surface of the solar panels.Therefore, it can also be described as a
set of photovoltaic modules, housed in a supporting structure. A photovoltaic (PV) module is an
integrated and connected assembly of solar cells. Solar panels wear very slowly. Annually, their performance decreases only by
about 2 per cent (sometimes, or less). Most solar panels are made using crystal solar cells.
But Loom Solar panels are made of Mono-PERC solar cells,
the latest type of solar cells commercially available in the market.
The installation of solar panels
in homes helps combat harmful greenhouse gas emissions and thus helps reduce global warming.
Solar panels do not lead to any kind of dirt and are clean.
It also reduces our reliance on (limited) energy and traditional energy sources.
